Advertisement company had problems tracking employes workload and performance. Unforchonetly current solutions on the market couldn't satisfy their needs 100%.

This application was developed from scratch.
It has a dashboard that allows displaying all current, past and coming advertising campaigns depending on user access level. There's a set of filters and charts that make analytics work a pleasure and great result!

Two months after it was launched client managed to allocate the workload in a better way. As a result, they took a couple more clients without hiring new employees!

Stack:
Front-End: ReactJs, Typescript, Redux, Redux-Saga.
Back-End: Node.js, Typescript, MySQL.
